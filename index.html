My Favourite Places that I have traveled to:

Banff

Banff National Park is well-known for its vibrant lakes, majestic mountains, and simple accessibility to a wide variety of outdoor activities. With many unique parks and places to see.

Punta Cana

Punta Cana is pure escape, and it offers a variety of pleasures. Days here may begin on the picture-perfect warm and sunny beach and end in a cave nightclub.

Argentina

Argentina is known for its love of Tango, Mate culture, and soccer. The country offers travelers a one-of-a-kind experience with stunning landscapes in Patagonia and city life in Buenos Aires.
